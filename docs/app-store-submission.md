## Description

Tennis Agent is a single-player management sim where you run a tennis agency from the ground up.

Scout new talent, sign rising juniors, manage senior pros, and make the calls that shape each season. Choose which tournaments your players enter, balance stamina and development, monitor injuries, and build careers that can turn into Grand Slam runs.

The game focuses on long-term agency management instead of on-court controls:

- Scout territories and recruit new players
- Manage junior and senior rosters
- Enter tournaments based on ranking and eligibility
- Simulate match results across a full season
- Track rankings, points, earnings, titles, and career milestones
- Train players and run exhibition matches
- Navigate random career events, endorsement deals, injuries, and roster turnover
- Continue your save with automatic on-device persistence

Tennis Agent is designed as an offline single-player experience. There are no accounts, no ads, and no paywalls.
