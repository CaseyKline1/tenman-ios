Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Tennis Agent is a single-player game. It stores your career progress only on your device so your save can be restored when you reopen the app.

  • No account is required to play.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party marketing SDKs are used by the app.
  • Your manager name, roster, and career progress are saved locally on-device.
  • Your save data is not uploaded to the developer and is not shared with third parties.
  • You can erase your save at any time by using the in-app retire option or by deleting the app.

Data Handling

Tennis Agent does not collect personal data for tracking, advertising, or analytics. The app uses local storage exclusively to keep your game progress available between launches.
